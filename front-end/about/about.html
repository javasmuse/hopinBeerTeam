About HOPin

Listen up, grab your pitcher, tune into your Ale-ment and let us inform you about the greatest full stack app developed in an Amazon SDE Apprenticeship. HOPin isn't just a cool, catchy, punny name of our application. It's a lifestyle. HOPin is the all inclusive one stop pub to adventure out and discover new beers. If you're of age of course. HOPin also let's you create a favorite's list based off of our recommended beers. Don't see your beer? Don't worry, this isn't shakesbeerean notation, it's simple. Simply add it to your list via our Favorites tab. Our service was brewed using a numerous amount of resources. The front of house and back of house is all managed using Javascript. We utilized precision design techniques of CSS3 to fit our overall theme, with HTML 5 being the blueprint of it all. Various outside vendors contribute to the success of HOPin. Source control using git, node packet manager handling our dependencies, axios maneuvering our CRUD operations, and many others like express, CORS managing, and mongodb. The team here is very excited for you to explore HOPin, have a brewtiful day.
